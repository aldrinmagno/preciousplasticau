About Precious Plastic Darwin

We're part of the global Precious Plastic movement, bringing innovative plastic recycling solutions to Darwin and the Northern Territory.

Our mission is to empower communities to tackle plastic waste through education, hands-on workshops, and collaborative projects that turn trash into treasure.

Join us in creating a more sustainable future, one plastic bottle at a time.
